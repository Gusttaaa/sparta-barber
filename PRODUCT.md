# SPARTA BARBER

## Users
- **Primary**: Men seeking premium haircuts, beard grooming, and professional barber services in Rio de Janeiro
- **Secondary**: First-time clients discovering the barbershop through the website
- **Context**: Urban professionals, lifestyle-conscious customers, tech-savvy millennial/Gen Z males

## Product Purpose
Premium barbershop brand website showcasing three locations (Recreio, Barra, Guanabara) in Rio de Janeiro. The site drives bookings, displays services/pricing, builds brand trust through portfolio galleries, and creates membership value through the "Clube" program.

## Brand Voice & Tone
- **Persona**: Elite, understated, confident—like the barbershop experience itself
- **Tone**: Sophisticated yet approachable. Professional mastery without pretension. Speaks to quality and craft.
- **Anti-references**: Corporate sterility, overstyled/trendy design, artificial warmth, generic SaaS landing pages

## Strategic Principles
1. **Performance first**: Fast loading and smooth interactions maintain premium perception
2. **Craftsmanship visible**: Every detail reflects the precision of the barber's work
3. **Trust building**: Galleries, testimonials, and professional presentation drive booking confidence
4. **Simplicity**: Navigation and booking flows remove friction; no unnecessary complexity
5. **Local pride**: Three locations, 10+ years of service emphasized; Rio's identity subtle but present

## Register
**Brand** — the landing page IS the brand experience. Design drives perception and conversions.
